Intersect.Ninja for Spotify

Looking for music everyone you're hanging out with will enjoy? Use Intersect.Ninja to find the artists and tracks that you and your friends have in common in your public playlists.

To get started, connect with your Spotify account.

Connect to Spotify

No information you provide to Intersect.Ninja (including your Spotify account info) is ever stored, since all processing is done locally on your device. Intersect.Ninja is open source (MIT License), so you can check out the project's source code on on GitHub. Intersect.Ninja is not in any way associated with or endosed by Spotify.
